# Final Review Action Plan

_Step-by-step live meeting preparation and follow-up checklist._

## Before the meeting

1. Send written notice of the meeting and agenda to all four initial directors: Send, Maeyee, Michael, and Fatih. Save screenshots/emails as proof.
2. Send the meeting materials to Maeyee, Fatih, Alp, and Timucin; also send to Michael if you have a working contact.
3. Get Alp Kirec and Timucin Erkan to reply or sign Consent to Serve forms and Conflict Disclosure forms.
4. Confirm Fatih for Sunday 9 PM Turkey time (or the agreed time) and calendar it.
5. Prepare a folder named SuperNova Board Records with subfolders: Certificate, Bylaws, Minutes, Consents, Conflicts, Policies, Name Change, Michael Outreach, Fatih Transition, Bank, IRS.
6. Have your name-change documentation and Certificate of Incorporation ready for the records binder.
7. Open the Short Meeting Script and read from it. Do not improvise the legal sequence.

## During the meeting

1. Start with Send, Maeyee, and Fatih as the quorum of the four initial directors. Alp and Timucin are guests at the start.
2. Confirm everyone can hear and participate.
3. Say the documents were circulated and ask if anyone requests a full reading. If nobody asks, waive the full reading.
4. Adopt the Bylaws.
5. Fix the authorized board size at six.
6. Appoint Alp Kirec and Timucin Erkan as directors.
7. Ask Fatih to confirm his voluntary resignation now that Alp and Timucin have been appointed.
8. Accept Fatih's resignation and thank him.
9. After Fatih leaves, confirm continuing quorum: four directors present out of a six-member entire board, until the next resolution.
10. Fix the board size at five.
11. State Michael remains a director unless he resigns or is later removed by lawful process.
12. Elect officers: Send as President; Maeyee as Secretary and Treasurer.
13. Approve internal roles: Timucin Erkan as Director of Systems and Operations; Alp Kirec as Director of Art and Creative Programs.
14. Adopt conflict, compensation, banking, fiscal year, EIN/tax-exemption, platform governance, and records binder resolutions.
15. Adjourn.

## After the meeting

2. Export/save the final minutes as PDF and DOCX.
3. Save proof of Fatih's attendance and/or verbal confirmation notes.
4. Collect signatures/electronic approvals for minutes and forms.
5. Save all forms: Alp consent, Timucin consent, conflict disclosures, conflict policy, compensation policy, Platform Governance Memo.
6. Continue outreach to Michael. Do not mark him as resigned until he clearly resigns.
7. Use the board records to open the bank account and complete/confirm EIN and tax-exemption filings.
8. Schedule a later follow-up board meeting to handle Michael cleanly if needed.
