The meeting materials were circulated in advance. Does anyone request a full reading of the documents? Hearing no request for a full reading, the full reading is waived.

### Purpose of meeting

The purpose of this meeting is to organize the Corporation, adopt bylaws, appoint active directors, handle Fatih Guler's voluntary director transition, elect officers, adopt governance policies, authorize financial and tax matters, and establish the corporate records binder.

### Adopt bylaws

I move that the Bylaws circulated to the Board be adopted as the Bylaws of SuperNova 2177 Inc. and filed with the corporate records. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Fix board size at six

I move that, pursuant to the Bylaws, the authorized number of directors of the Corporation be fixed at six, creating two newly created directorships. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Appoint Alp and Timucin

Alp Kirec and Timucin Erkan have each consented to serve as Directors and have disclosed any conflicts or confirmed that no conflicts are known. I move that Alp Kirec and Timucin Erkan be appointed as Directors of SuperNova 2177 Inc., effective immediately. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Fatih transition

Fatih, can you confirm that you voluntarily resign as a Director of SuperNova 2177 Inc., effective now that Alp Kirec and Timucin Erkan have been appointed as Directors? I move that the Board accept Fatih Guler's voluntary resignation and thank him for serving as an initial founding Director and helping with this transition. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Continuing quorum after Fatih leaves

Following Fatih Guler's resignation, the authorized number of directors remains six until the next resolution. The directors then serving are Send, Maeyee, Michael McCloud, Alp Kirec, and Timucin Erkan, with one vacancy. The directors present are Send, Maeyee, Alp, and Timucin. Four directors are present, which is a quorum of the six-member entire board.

### Fix board size at five

I move that the authorized number of directors of the Corporation be fixed at five, so no vacancy remains. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Michael status

Michael McCloud remains a Director unless and until he resigns, is removed in accordance with law, or another lawful vacancy event occurs. I move that the Secretary continue outreach to Michael McCloud to confirm whether he wishes to continue serving or resign, and preserve all communications in the corporate records. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Officers and program roles

I move that Send Kim SyNk Tae-Ha AE-Connection be elected President, Maeyee Kim be elected Secretary and Treasurer, Timucin Erkan be designated Director of Systems and Operations, and Alp Kirec be designated Director of Art and Creative Programs. These internal program titles do not create compensation rights or independent authority to bind the Corporation except as approved by the Board or authorized officers. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Policies and other organizational actions

I move that the Conflict of Interest Policy, Compensation and Reimbursement Policy, fiscal year, banking authorization, EIN/tax-exemption authorization, Platform Governance Memo, and corporate records binder resolution be adopted as circulated. Do I have a second? All in favor?

### Adjourn

If there is no further business, I move to adjourn the meeting. Do I have a second? All in favor? The meeting is adjourned at __________.
